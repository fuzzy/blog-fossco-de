* Emacs Cheat Sheets

*** General

*** T.H.W.A.P.

***** Elfeed

| Emacs key combo | What it does                        |
|-----------------+-------------------------------------|
| C-t r s         | Starts the Elfeed RSS reader        |
| C-t r u         | Updates the local cache of stories  |
| C-t r f         | Set what filters are in current use |

***** Magit

| Emacs key combo | What it does                                 |
|-----------------+----------------------------------------------+
| C-t g s         | Runs git status via Magit (M-x magit-status) |
